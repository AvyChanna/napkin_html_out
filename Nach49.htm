46  Algebraic integers

Here’s a first taste of algebraic number theory.

This is really close to the border between olympiads and higher math. You’ve always known that a + b had a “norm” a2 − 2b2, and that somehow this norm was multiplicative. You’ve also always known that roots come in conjugate pairs. You might have heard of minimal polynomials but not know much about them.

This chapter and the next one will make all these vague notions precise. It’s drawn largely from the first chapter of [?].
