Something curious happens:

  • When (p) is lifted into KD it splits completely into g unramified primes. Each of these has inertial degree 1.
  • When the primes in KD are lifted to KI, they remain inert, and now have inertial degree f.
  • When then lifted to K, they ramify with exponent e (but don’t split at all).
