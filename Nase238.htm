36.3  A few harder problems to think about

Problem 36A⋆ (The indicator of the rationals). Take the indicator function 1ℚ : ℝ →{0,1}⊆ ℝ for the rational numbers.

(a)
Prove that 1ℚ is not Riemann integrable.
(b)
Show that ∫ ℝ1ℚ exists and determine its value — the one you expect!
