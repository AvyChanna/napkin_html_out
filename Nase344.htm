We also have an important lemma:

Lemma 55.1.3 (Order of the Frobenius element)
Let Frob𝔭 be a Frobenius element from an extension K∕ℚ. Then the order of 𝔭 is equal to the inertial degree f𝔭. In particular, (p) splits completely in 𝒪K if and only if Frob𝔭 = id.
