Problem 6C† (Completion of a metric space).     Let M be a metric space. Construct a complete metric space M such that M is a subspace of M, and every open set of M contains a point of M (meaning M is dense in M).

Problem 6D. Show that a metric space is totally bounded if and only if any sequence has a Cauchy subsequence.
