More generally, suppose V is an irrep and consider Homrep(V ⊕m,V ⊕n). Intertwining operators are determined completely T : V ⊕m → V ⊕n by the mn choices of compositions where the first arrow is inclusion to the ith component of V ⊕m (for 1 ≤ i ≤ m) and the second arrow is inclusion to the jth component of V ⊕n (for 1 ≤ j ≤ n). However, by Schur’s lemma on each of these compositions, we know they must be constant.

Corollary 20.1.3 (Subrepresentations of completely reducible representations)
 Let V = ⊕ V i⊕ni be completely reducible. Then any subrepresentation W of V is isomorphic to ⊕ V i⊕mi where mi ≤ ni for each i, and the inclusion WV is given by the direct sum of inclusion V i⊕miV i⊕ni, which are ni × mi matrices.
